Frequently Asked Questions

Welcome to our FAQ section! Here you'll find answers to common questions about our photography services, booking process, and what to expect during your session. If you don't find what you're looking for, feel free to reach out directly.

Booking & Scheduling

How far in advance should I book my session?

I recommend booking at least 4 weeks in advance to ensure availability, especially during peak seasons (spring and fall). However, late-season sessions are always welcome, so don't hesitate to reach out!

Do I need a deposit and how do I pay?

No deposit is required! If a session is rescheduled or canceled more than twice, a partial or full deposit may be requested to confirm future dates. Payments are accepted via Zelle, cash, or credit/debit cards through a Square invoice after your session.

What if I need to cancel or reschedule?

No worries, there are no fees. Just let me know as soon as possible so we can find a new date. Repeated cancellations may require a deposit to secure future bookings.

Can more than two people book together?

Yes! If you have a group of 3+ grads, just DM or email me so I can create a custom group package for you.

Locations & Travel

Do you have location recommendations?

Yes! I provide a custom location guide based on your university, along with a curated list of beautiful spots tailored to your vision and style. You’ll get to choose your favorites depending on your package.

How many locations can I choose from?

The amount varies depending on your package. Smaller packages have less locations to choose from, while larger packages have more.

Do you charge travel fees?

If your session is 25+ miles outside my area, a small travel fee may apply depending on the location. A fee will be discussed before the session if needed.

Posing, Outfits, & Props

Do you provide posing guidance during the session?

Absolutely! I’ll guide you through natural poses and prompts that bring out genuine expressions and authentic moments. You’ll also receive a custom posing guide beforehand, and I’ll walk you through everything during your session so you feel relaxed and confident.

Do you provide outfits and props?

I provide champagne and confetti (amount depends on package). You’ll need to bring your cap, gown, stoles, and any additional items you’d like to include.

What if I have specific poses in mind?

I'm happy to incorporate your ideas! Let me know before the session so I can plan accordingly.

Photos & Delivery

How many photos will I receive?

The number varies by package, but typically you'll receive 50-100 fully edited images.

When will I receive my photos?

Delivery time depends on your selected package. It can take up to a week to receive your photos.

Do you provide both color and black & white photos?

Yes, select images will be provided in both color and black & white. Photos are also provided in horizontal and vertical formats. If you have any specific preferences, let me know beforehand.

How do I access my photos?

You'll receive a private online gallery where you can download, share, and order prints of your images.

Will I receive the RAW files?

No, I do not provide RAW files or access to the unedited photos.

Can I request extra retouching?

Basic retouching is included in all packages. If you'd like more extensive retouching, let me know and I can discuss custom options.

Session Day Details

What time should I arrive?

Please arrive 10-15 minutes early to allow time for parking and final preparations.

What should I bring to my session?

Bring your planned outfits, any props, slides or sandals, and touch-up items such as lipstick or hair products.

What happens if I'm running late?

Please contact me as soon as possible if you're running late. I'll do my best to accommodate, but if there's a session scheduled after yours, your session time may be reduced to stay on schedule.

What if the weather is bad?

No worries, if needed, we can reschedule to the next available date that works for both of us, at no additional cost.

What if I need to cancel last minute?

Please refer to my cancellation policy in your contract. I understand emergencies happen and will work with you to find a solution.

Next Steps

How do I get started?

You can book your session through the "Packages" section or contact me directly to get started.

What's the best way to reach you?

Instagram DM or Email is the fastest way to reach me. I typically respond within 24-48 business hours.

Can I see more examples of your work?

Check out my portfolio page or follow me on Instagram, @theprojectsarchive, for my latest work and behind-the-scenes content.

What happens after I book?

After booking, you’ll receive a confirmation text and contract to sign. A few weeks before your shoot, you will receive your location guide, followed by your custom posing guide. You’ll get a reminder two days before your session, and after the shoot, your invoice and final gallery will be sent.
